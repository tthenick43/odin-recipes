Cherry Coffee Cake

Ingredients

  • 3 cups cherry pie filling
  • 2 cups sugar
  • 3 cups all purpose flower
  • 1 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon baking soda
  • 12 tablespoons butter
  • 2 beaten eggs
  • 1 cup buttermilk
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla
  • 1/2 cup all purpose flower
  1. In a mixing bowl stir together 1 cup sugar, the 3 cups of flour, the baking powder, and baking soda. Cut in 8 tablespoon butter until mixture resembles fine crumbs. Combine eggs, buttermilk, and vanilla. Add flour mixture and stur until moistened
  2. Spread half of the batter into a 8x17 baking pan. Spread the pie filling over batter. Drop remaining batter into small mounds atop filling
  3. Combine the remaining 1/2 cup sugar and 1/2 cup flour. Cut in remaining 4 tablespoon of butter until mixture resembles fine crumbs. Sprinkle ontop of coffee cake
  4. Bake @ 350 for 45 minutes until golden brown in color.
